\# Orwell Reference Notes



Main reference: https://orwell.byholm.co/



Use as interaction/storytelling reference only. Do not copy Orwell text, images, branding, exact code, or red propaganda theme.



What works:

\- It feels like a scrolling presentation/story, not a normal website.

\- Full-screen scenes.

\- Minimal text per scene.

\- Strong typography.

\- Cursor-reactive objects.

\- Scene transitions with static/glitch.

\- Images rotate or move with cursor.

\- Text types/reveals in sequence.

\- Pinned sections while scroll drives animation.

\- Final quote/ending feels cinematic.

\- It has no normal app function, but it still feels complete and intentional.
